How to Use the CardFindz eBay Calculator

This calculator helps sports card and collectible resellers calculate their true profit after eBay fees and costs. Enter your sale price, item cost, and shipping cost to see your real profit instantly.

eBay Fee Structure

  • eBay Final Value Fee: 13.25% of the sale price + $0.30 per transaction
  • Payment Processing: 2.9% of the sale price + $0.30 per transaction
  • Total eBay Fees: Approximately 16.15% of your sale price + $0.60

Why Use an eBay Profit Calculator?

Many card resellers lose money without realizing it because they don't account for all eBay fees. Between final value fees, payment processing, and shipping, eBay fees can eat up 16-20% of your sale price. This calculator shows you exactly where your money goes and helps you price cards correctly to ensure profit.

Tips for Maximizing Profit on eBay

  • Factor in ALL costs before listing - including toploaders, team bags, and bubble mailers
  • Use calculated shipping to avoid losing money on heavier packages
  • Aim for at least 30-50% profit margin to make card flipping worthwhile
  • Consider eBay Promoted Listings cost (2-10% additional) if you use them
  • Bundle cards when possible to spread fixed fees across multiple items
  • Price competitively but don't undercut yourself - use comps wisely

Common Costs for Card Resellers

Supplies: Toploaders ($0.10-0.30), team bags ($0.02-0.05), bubble mailers ($0.50-1.00), tape and labels

Shipping: First Class (up to 13oz): $4-5, Priority Mail: $8-10, tracking included

Storage: Card boxes, binders, storage space for inventory

Grading (Optional): PSA ($20-150), BGS ($20-400), can significantly increase value if done right
